Hello!

My name is Kimberly Flores

I am an aspiring front-end designer



I am a recent graduate from the University of California, Irvine(UCI) where I obtained a B.S. in Informatics with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction.

Besides designing and coding, I love to help the people in my community. I have had multiple positions on the campus which advocates for helping first-year students transition to the university. I think it is a beautiful thing to see people grow through the advice they have taken. Having conversations with them and learning how they changed from the beginning of the school year to the end, is just mind-blowing.

My passion for wanting to help others and understanding their needs, helps me when I create designs for technologies. It allows me to make these beautiful user-centric designs tailored for them.

If you have any opportunities for me, feel free to send me a message!
